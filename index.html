About Me

  • Online usernames: tirimid, Tirimid, polytopelover
  • Languages I understand: English (fluent), Russian (fluent), Spanish (limited)
  • Some things I do: programming, writing, music

Hello, I'm tirimid (sometimes Tirimid or polytopelover) and this is my personal website for publishing things I make - which means essays, guides, documentation and so on. Whenever I create something that I want to put out into the world for others to see, I will probably create a page about it on this site. Obviously not everything I've ever made is something I'm comfortable with uploading here, so this is not a definitive catalog of my works.

I am strongly in favor of free software, privacy, and security. The source code to this website is available at its GitHub.

If you need to contact me for some reason, you can send an email to tirimid@tirimid.net. I usually check this mailbox at least once a day, but do not expect an immediate response.

About me

Hello, I'm tirimid (sometimes Tirimid or polytopelover) and this is my personal website for publishing things I make. Obviously not everything I've ever made will be present here, so this is not a definitive catalog of my works.

If you need to contact me for some reason, you can send an email to tirimid@tirimid.net. I usually check this mailbox at least once a day, but do not expect an immediate response.

I also have a YouTube channel and a page on itch.io.

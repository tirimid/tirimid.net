Usage

Eye tool: click near the edges of the popup to move the camera
Box tool: drag to draw platform in that region
Kill tool: drag to draw kill blocks in that region
Spawnpoint tool: click to move the spawnpoint
Goal tool: drag to draw a goal for players to reach
Coin tool: add a coin which incentivizes players to move to it
Eraser tool: click something to erase it
Train AI: start the simulation with current parameters
Kill AI: terminate the simulation
